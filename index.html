Who We Are

More Than A
Label.

Off-Limits Entertainment was born in Abuja, Nigeria from a refusal to accept the status quo. We are a full-service entertainment company — part record label, part creative agency, part cultural movement. Inspired by the blueprints of Roc Nation, 88rising, and Top Dawg Entertainment, we've built a machine that doesn't just sign artists — it builds legacies.

From A&R and production to brand partnerships, touring, and film — every arm of Off-Limits is engineered to amplify your voice and expand your world.
